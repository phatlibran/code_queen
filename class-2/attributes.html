Serena Jameka Williams, href ="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Serena_Williams" born September 26, 1981, is an American professional tennis player. Williams was born in Saginaw, Michigan and is the youngest of five daughters including her sister, Venus Williams href ="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Venus_Williams" . When the children were young, the family moved to href ="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compton,_California" Compton, Californi where Williams started playing tennis at the age of four. In addition to being a tennis superstar, Serena is a model, activist, entertainer, philanthropist and author. Williams released her first solo autobiography entitled On the Line href ="https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/6625370-on-the-line" , following the 2009 US Open.
